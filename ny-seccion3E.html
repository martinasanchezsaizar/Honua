STAY

Bowery: You traveling to New York on a budget but want to be in the middle of it all? Then check out our Grand Bowery Hotel located in the heart of china town. Minutes away from Little Italy, downtown shopping, and right around the corner from Williamsburg Bridge.

Radio City is in downtown New York, a 15-minute walk from Lincoln Square. The budget hotel in New York draws guests with Italian architecture. This hotel has free Wi-Fi.

The Local: this ultrahip hostel is located in a renovated industrial building overlooking the Manhattan skyline, a 4-minute walk from the nearest subway station, and a 9-minute walk from the MoMA PS1 Museum of Modern Art.

EAT

La fondita restaurant is a Mexican Restaurant where you will eat well, and don't spend much. If you are trying to escape from NYC just for a little, you found the right place!

Papa's Kitchen is a cornerstone in the Jackson Heights community and has been recognized for its outstanding Asian cuisine, excellent service, and friendly staff.

Yu Kitchen: This space includes booths, and you can spend $25 per person on a shared meal with Shanghai, Sichuan, Shaanxi, and Cantonese specialties
