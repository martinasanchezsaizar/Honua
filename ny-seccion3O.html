STAY

The Roosevelt Hotel has been a landmark on the Manhattan skyline since it opened on September 22, 1924. Over the last 90+ years, this luxurious hotel has remained a true icon of New York City, welcoming millions of guests from around the world.

Doubletree by Hilton: Located in a building in the historic Stone Street district of Lower Manhattan, this elegant hotel is a 5-minute walk from Wall Street, a 7-minute walk from The Battery Park and a 15-minute walk from One World Trade Center.

ROW nyc has pioneered a unique type of hotel, meeting NYC’s urban grit with grandeur and offering a completely contemporary experience with an incomparable front-row seat at Times Square.
