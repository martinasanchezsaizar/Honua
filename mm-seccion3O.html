STAY

The Mara Big Five Camp is located right at the Talek river, just a few minutes away from the Maasai Mara. Eight tents with the well known rustic safari-style are at the guests’ disposal.

Duma Bush Safari Camp is situated in the stunning Maasai Mara National Reserve, this private camp promises guests a holiday of a lifetime!

Kobe House is a guest house bordering Maasai Mara Game Reserve and Olare Motorogi Conservancy, 6km north of Talek Gate. Come and experience the breathtaking views of the Mara plains as you enjoy top of the class service in our high standard facilities

ACTIVITIES

The safari is included in where you stay!

Birdwatching: The Masai Mara isn’t one of Kenya’s birding hotspots. However, with more than 500 bird species recorded, this isn’t a bad place to mark off a lot of Kenya’s savannah species from your bird list.

Horseback safari: The stables are situated along the border of Olare Motorogi Conservancy and Masai Mara National Reserve. The two camps that are nearest the stables are Great Plains’ Mara Plains Camp and Mara Expedition Camp. Each of these camps is within two kilometers of the stables, but guests from other Masai Mara camps and lodges are welcome.
