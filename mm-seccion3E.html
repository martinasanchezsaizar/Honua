STAY

Masai Mara Explorers Backpakers Camp. If you are just looking for simplicity and a backpacker-friendly place, this might be your ideal spot.

Osim Camp. Located just 17 kilometers after Narok, this is the cheapest campsite in the Maasai Mara and the reason is that it is a bit far from the main gates to the park, around 70 kilometers.

Massai Village and Safari: This is a different experience because it is the house of a local Maasai, so it is basically a homestay.

TIPS AND RECCOMENDATIONS

Buy supplies before on the supermarket. On the camps, it is everything more expensive, this is a useful tip if you really want to do this trip on budget.

Safari tours. The camps recommended you includes safari experiences.

Take a matatu (loca transport) to the town. If you really want to do this experience on a budget you will have go by local transportation.
