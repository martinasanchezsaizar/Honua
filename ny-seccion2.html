When to go?

The best time to visit NY is anytime! Central Park is beautifully dressed in white as well as in full bloom. I love to go in October and see al the spring vibe.

Transportation

I promise that by taking the taxi you will be late! NYC's public transit system is the easiest way to get around. The metro card costs only $2.75 a ride and for a seven - day unlimited MetroCard costs $31

Weather

January is the coolest month with an average temperature of 36°F (2.2°F). July is the hottest month with an average temperature of 83°F (28.9°C).

Know before visiting

You are going to walk al the city! Don't forget to wear comfortable cloth and sophisticated as well, of course!

Language

Yes, NYC is a cosmopolitan city, there are people from all over the world. The language that they use is English.

Electric

Type A (two-prong plug) or Type B (three-prong plug)
