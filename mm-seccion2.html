When to visit

The best time to visit Masai Mara is during the dry season from late June to October (these are the best wildlife viewing months), says an renowed Africa expert, Anthony Ham.

Weather

The warmest month is March with an average maximum temperature of 28°C (82°F). The coldest month is January with an average maximum temperature of 24°C (44°F).

Know before you go

There are compulsory vaccinations required for entry to Kenya, in which case a Certificate of Inoculation against Yellow Fever is required from travelers older than one year. Most doctors recommend vaccinating against Polio, Tetanus, Hepatitis and Yellow Fever.

Language

English and Swahili

Electric

In Kenya the power plugs and sockets are of type G. The standard voltage is 240 V and the standard frequency is 50 Hz.
